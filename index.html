Take the nightsky home

There aren't many things more beautiful than a clear sky full of stars. Even though it's night 50% of the time, we rarely take the time to admire our beautiful sky. Now, you can take the nightsky everywhere you go!

How we bring the nightsky to you

The home planetarium is 3D-printed from recycled PLA.

The 300 brightest stars are at their position in a celestial sphere.

Brighter stars shine brighter through bigger holes.

Rechargable and adjustable to two brightness settings.
